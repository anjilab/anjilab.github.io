Anjila Budathoki is a Ph.D. student at Georgia State University, supervised by Dr. Yi Ding, and works as a Graduate Research Assistant. Her research focuses lies on intersection of AI safety and understanding how humans perceive and are affected by AI systems. Currently, she works on AI safety benchmarking in model compression techniques and is interested in studying human simulation via LLMs and identifying gaps in existing approaches.
